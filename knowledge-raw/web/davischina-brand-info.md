# Davis Brand Info (from davischina.com)

## Company History
- AL DAVIS entered pet industry in 1969
- Founded DAVIS company in 1982
- Family enterprise with nearly 40 years of history
- Mission: "Helping pets stay clean"
- Grew from family business to large enterprise
- Products sold worldwide in 31+ countries

## Product Lines
- 32+ unique formulas, all professional grade, premium ingredients
- Four main series:
  1. Salon Series - efficient daily grooming for pet stores
  2. Show Beauty Series - high-end show competition grooming
  3. Skin Care Series - veterinary-grade skin health
  4. Ear Care Series - ear hygiene and mite prevention

## Achievements
- First US shampoo brand in veterinary channel
- Famous "Four Brothers" skin care lineup
- FCI World Dog Federation judge Victor M Rosado recommends
- 2018/2019 Westminster Best of Breed champion (Tibetan Mastiff EVO) uses Show Beauty series
- CFA website senior breeder section recommends Degrease Shampoo
